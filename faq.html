Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Closed on Sundays.

Do you deliver?

Yes! We deliver within a 10-mile radius. Orders can be placed online through our Delivery page.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Absolutely. We have a selection of gluten-free and vegan baked goods available daily.

How can I place a custom cake order?

You can fill out the order form on the Delivery page or call us directly. Custom orders should be made at least 48 hours in advance.

Do you offer catering for events?

Yes, we offer catering for all types of events including birthdays, weddings, and corporate functions. Contact us for more details.
