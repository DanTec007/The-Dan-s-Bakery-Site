About Us

Welcome to Dan's Bakery, where tradition meets flavor! Established in 1995, Dan's Bakery has been serving freshly baked goods with love and dedication. What started as a small family-owned shop has grown into a beloved neighborhood staple for delicious treats and warm smiles.

Our Mission

We aim to bring joy to every customer by providing the freshest baked products made with natural ingredients. Our mission is rooted in quality, customer satisfaction, and a genuine passion for baking.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate bakers, friendly front staff, and creative minds. Meet our founder Dan Rivera, a master baker with over 30 years of experience. Alongside Dan, our talented team works day and night to bring your favorite pastries, cakes, and breads to life.

Our History

From our humble beginnings in a small kitchen to becoming a local favorite, Dan's Bakery has always stayed true to its roots. Over the years, we have expanded our menu, embraced new baking technologies, and maintained a close-knit connection with our community.
